DESCRIPTION

Les Infusions de Prada reveal unexpected facets of iconic ingredients in second-skin fragrances. In all Les Infusions, a very pure base called “esprit d’infusion” (made of aldehydes, musks and citrus) allows a star ingredient to fully express itself. A collection of sophisticated fragrances offering the most complete fusion between you and your fragrance. In fusion with you.
