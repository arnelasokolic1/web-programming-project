PRADA/LUNA ROSSA

LUNA ROSSA DEO SPRAY 150ML

85KM

DESCRIPTION

The Prada Luna Rossa fragrances are inspired by the world of extreme sailing competition, an ode to high performance through high innovation. Prada Luna Rossa Eau de Toilette is an energizing men's fragrance, a fougere aromatic scent combining unexpected ingredients: fresh lavender, a vibrant spearmint and an intense amber.
Prada Luna Rossa Eau de Toilette evokes the performance with a team through intense competition. A fragrance for a competitive man in pursuit of excellence.
Prada Luna Rossa Eau de Toilette perfume bottle is inspired by the technological, dynamic lines of extreme sailing boats. The intense silver is balanced with the iconic Prada red line, as a symbol of the franchise.
